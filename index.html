About Castro Carpentry

With over 20 years of experience in the woodworking industry, Castro Carpentry has become a trusted name in Magnolia, Texas. We specialize in both interior and exterior carpentry services, delivering exceptional quality and attention to detail on every project.

Our commitment to professionalism, punctuality, and customer satisfaction has earned us the trust of countless homeowners throughout the Houston area. From custom kitchens to outdoor decks, we bring your vision to life with precision and care.
